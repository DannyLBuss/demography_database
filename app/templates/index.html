Welcome!

COMPADRE and COMADRE contain matrix population models of hundreds of plant and animal species.

Matrix population models:

  • summarize the life cycle components of survival, growth and reproduction
  • acknowledge size or age variation of individuals within a population
  • can be used to compare traits across species and populations
  • can be used to calculate a multitude of demographic traits: including, population growth rates, mean life expectancies and reproductive rates of each stage class
